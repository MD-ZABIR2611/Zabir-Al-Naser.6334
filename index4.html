Mymensingh - The City of Heritage

Mymensingh is one of the oldest and most culturally rich cities in Bangladesh, located on the bank of the Brahmaputra River. It's known for its historical significance, educational institutions, and natural beauty.

The city has a rich heritage dating back to the Mughal period and was an important administrative center during British rule. Today, it's a major educational hub with agricultural university and medical college attracting students from all over the country.

Mymensingh is surrounded by beautiful landscapes, including the famous Bangladesh Agricultural University campus which is considered one of the most beautiful university campuses in Asia. The city also has several historical sites, parks, and cultural centers that reflect its rich heritage.

Traditional crafts, particularly Nakshi Kantha (embroidered quilt), and folk music are integral parts of Mymensingh's cultural identity. The city hosts various cultural festivals throughout the year that showcase local traditions and arts.
